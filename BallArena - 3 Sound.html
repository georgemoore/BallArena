BallArena Mechanics

Kinematics

• Freeze: When enabled, contacts between balls (births, kills, or plain bounces) causes a momentary pause in time. Boundary collisions do NOT trigger a freeze.

Sonic Landscape

• Wall Bounce: High-pitched 'pip' based on speed.

• Birth: Rising sine-wave 'bloops'.

• Consumption: Low-frequency square-wave 'crunch'.

• Killers: Emit a flat, aggressive sawtooth tone when they strike.

Social Contract

• Infancy Shield: New births are protected for 60 ticks. Parents cannot eat their own direct children.
