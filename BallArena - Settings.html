Simulation Mechanics

Predation

When balls exceed the Evolution Trigger, they become Hunters. Hunters kill non-parents they touch, reset their "Hunter Mode" timer, and grow stronger with every kill.

Genetics

Offspring receive a color mix from parents and a health bonus, allowing the population to evolve towards higher longevity over many generations.

Stress

As the arena approaches its capacity, the environment becomes hostile. Every ball's lifespan is slashed based on the Lethality Multiplier to prevent overcrowding.
